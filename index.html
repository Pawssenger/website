About Us

Because every ride should be a paw-sitive one.

Pawssenger Pets Relocation Services L.L.C is a passionate and professional pet transportation company based in the UAE, officially launched in 2025. While we are new to the industry, our mission is driven by a deep love for animals and a strong commitment to their comfort and safety.

We know that pets are family — and we treat them like it. Our priority is to deliver a travel experience that is smooth, stress-free, and above all, caring. From pickup to drop-off, every pet receives personalized attention, compassion, and comfort throughout their journey.

As a licensed and registered company, we comply with all UAE regulations for pet transport. We pride ourselves on clear communication, transparency, and consistent service, offering peace of mind to every pet parent who entrusts us with their beloved companions.

At Pawssenger, we don’t just move pets — we create safe, reliable, and paw-sitive travel experiences. 🐾
