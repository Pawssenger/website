FAQs

1. What is Pawssenger?

A licensed pet taxi and relocation service operating across the UAE for all kinds of pets.

2. What animals do you transport?

We transport all pets — dogs, cats, birds, rabbits, reptiles, and more.

3. Do you serve all Emirates?

Yes, we operate in Dubai and to/from Sharjah, Abu Dhabi, RAK, Ajman, UAQ, Fujairah, and Al Ain.

4. How do I book?

Simply message us via WhatsApp or email us at booking@pawssenger.ae.

5. Can I join my pet? What are the requirements?

Yes! One pet parent may ride along (if space allows). Also, please provide a copy of your pet’s vaccination certificate — it’s required for safety and shared rides.

6. Is the car cleaned?

Yes, our vehicles are disinfected after each trip to ensure hygiene and safety.
